Everything you need to know about home ownership with Prehome.

27 AUG,2024     5 min read
273  

Welcome to Prehome, a company that helps you achieve your dream of homeownership by providing a unique renting experience.Â 

At Prehome, we understand that homeownership is a dream for many people. However, saving enough money for a down payment can be a challenging and time-consuming process. That's where Prehome comes in. We offer ready-to-move-in apartments for rent, which allows you to experience your dream home today while building your savings over the next two years to become a fully-eligible homeowner. The property's value is locked at the current market rate, which allows you to benefit from capital appreciation when you eventually purchase the house two years later.

Our mission is to help you achieve your dream of homeownership while providing you with the flexibility and convenience of renting. Our rental program allows you to live in your dream home while building up your savings and credit score, so you can eventually qualify for a mortgage and become a homeowner. Our team is dedicated to providing you with a seamless renting experience, with top-notch customer service and support.
